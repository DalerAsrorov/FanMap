FanMap is a web application that allows a user or an artist to see the newest trends in music, costs-benefits analysis results, and social network perception about a particular artist. This application is particularly useful for the mainstream artists because they will be able to see what countries, cities, or areas they can visit based on the public perception of the artist around the world. The app will give a detailed information of what are the possible costs, benefits, and probabillity of the sold-out venues based on the analysis taken from social media.
